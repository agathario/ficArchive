Upon dropping him off at the school office half an hour late, Agatha hugged Nicky goodbye and watched him walk away in his two different shoes that she had forgotten to correct.

She decided she needed to collect her thoughts before storming home and starting a screaming match with Ralph. She pulled into the parking lot of a quaint cafe that she had passed regularly on the way to school, but had never found the time to check it out.

A delightful bell jingled over head as she pushed open the door, the strong smell of coffee and cinnamon filling her lungs as a breath of warm, cozy air enveloped her.

The person behind the desk had a lovely smiling face and a friendly demeanour, making easy small talk with Agatha, a calming conversation about the snow outside and how cold it had been lately. Agatha felt better already.

Then, upon turning around with her coffee in hand, Agatha ran directly into the person behind her, spilling boiling hot coffee all over herself.

“Oh my god, I’m so sorry!” The person squeaked, leaning around Agatha to grab a huge handful of tissues and roughly dabbing at Agatha’s torso.

“Agatha.”
