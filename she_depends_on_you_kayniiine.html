"Yeah? Worth it how?" Rio posed back, cocking a brow. When Agatha only answered with an overexaggerated shrug of her shoulders, Rio kept pressing, though she tried another avenue. "If you won't tell me that, will you at least tell me how much you raked in this week?"

The snort that sounded from Agatha almost startled Rio, but not because it was her chosen reaction. No, it startled her because it meant that whatever she was about to say about going to sound completely and utterly insane. 

And it did.

"All in all, with this five grand, I believe the total sits at one-hundred-and-four thousand dollars, give or take."

Rio sucked in a breath so sharp that it dislodged a glob of split from her teeth and choked her, causing her to cough and sputter at Agatha's side, her unoccupied hand flying up to grasp at her throat as though it would have eased the choking sensation.

"104 thousand dollars, Agatha?! What the fuck do we need that kind of money for?!" She was clearly disbelieving, her head whipping from Agatha's profile to the path before them to ensure she would not run into anything.

"We don't need it," Agatha returned. "But there's something I want it for. And I think you're going to want it, too."

This answer was a perplexing one, and it showed on Rio's face as she continued to steal glances at her wife. "Yeah? And what might that be?"

Agatha simply shrugged, but the grin that filled her face a second later was damn near as bright as the sun.

"I suppose you'll have to wait until we get home to find out."

"That's tomorrow, though. How am I supposed to not think about it until then?" Rio whined back, her lip folding out in a pout.

Agatha, still not slowing their pace, leaned over just enough to provide Rio with a kiss that was more of a threat than anything else, even for how short it was.

"Oh, my love, I've got plenty of other things for you to think about. I had a lot of time to think about what I wanted to do to you on our last night when I was locked up." Agatha's voice was serious, but Rio knew her, knew that she was being... well, so very Agatha.

"Oh, so does that mean I get to fuck a hardened criminal tonight?" 

"If that's what gets those juices flowing baby, I can be as hardened or softened as you need."

Rio's laughter was bright and shrill as she rolled her eyes and shook her head.

"Hey Agatha?" she posed, their hotel coming into view.

"Hm?" she offered back sweetly.

"You're an idiot. And I love you."

"I love you too, my idiot."
