"Look, I know we haven't exactly seen eye to eye, but Rio is my best friend and I would do anything to protect her."

Agatha rolled her eyes.

And she would quite gladly kick Wanda's ass.

"And I take it from what you said through the door, and from the fact that my best friend can be completely oblivious to certain things, that she doesn't know?"

Agatha scoffed.

"I barely even know!"

The words came out sharper than she'd intended.

"It wasn't until you and Lilia were making creepy faces at me that I even started to suspect."

Wanda simply nodded, fiddling with her fingers for a moment before glancing down at the box in Agatha's hands.

God, she was annoying.

The silence stretched.

"So..." Wanda said carefully. "Are you gonna—"

Agatha let out an exasperated noise and threw both hands into the air.

"Fine! Fucking... just wait here."

She turned on her heel and stomped down the hallway towards the bathroom.

The door slammed behind her with enough force to rattle the frame.

She was acting like a fucking teenager.

So sue her.

She hated this whole situation.

She hated not knowing.

She hated the possibility that Wanda might be right.

And if she was being completely honest with herself, Wanda's presence wasn't helping either.

Her territorial omega was already worked up, and having Rio's ex sitting in her living room while she took a pregnancy test was pushing it dangerously close to its limit.

Life was bullshit.

For three painstaking minutes, Agatha had completely reorganised her bathroom cabinet twice, glancing every few seconds at the little white stick perched on the edge of the bathtub like it was personally mocking her.

She now kinda wished Wanda had brought a bomb.

At least that would've been straightforward.

The shrill chime of her phone timer made her jump.

"Jesus fucking Christ."

Instead of walking over and checking it immediately, she froze.

One arm crossed protectively over her abdomen while the other came up to her mouth, chewing anxiously at her thumbnail as she glared at the little white stick sitting a few feet away.

Taunting her.

Fuck.

This was life-changing shit.

She could feel sweat gathering at her temples and her breathing had started to pick up.

Her heart was beating so fucking hard she could practically hear it.

Wanda had already all but confirmed she was pregnant.

But hearing it was one thing, but actually seeing it in plain English?

That felt real.

Agatha squeezed her eyes shut and started walking towards the bathtub taking one tentative step at a time.

She only stopped when her toe smacked into the side of it.

"Motherfucker."

Taking a shaky breath, she opened her eyes and stared straight ahead at the wall tiles, noticing the build up of limescale she kept meaning to scrub.

Anything to delay this for another few seconds.

Because once she looked, there was no taking it back.

Fuck.

Fuck, fuck, fuck.

Slowly, she lowered her gaze.

And instantly found herself staring at the one word she had never imagined applying to her.

Pregnant.

The bathroom fell completely silent as Agatha stared.

Then stared some more, waiting for the word to change or the test to realise it had made a mistake. 

The word remained exactly where it was.

Pregnant.

“Holy fucking shit.”

...

The next thing Agatha knew, she was being lightly slapped on the face and staring into complete darkness.

“Agatha, wake up. Can you hear me?”

Fucking Wanda. 

“Stop touching me!”

Swatting away the offending hands from her face, she sat up and abruptly took in her surroundings. 

She was lying on the cold tile floor of her bathroom,Wanda crouched down beside her looking worried. Her hands were raised in surrender now, hovering uncertainly nearby as if she wasn’t entirely convinced Agatha wasn’t about to keel over again.

“You fainted.”

Yeah, no shit, Sherlock. 

"Ya think?" Agatha snapped as she pushed herself upright.

Immediately Wanda reached out to steady her as Agatha swatted her away again.

"Come on," Wanda sighed. "You need something to eat and drink. I boiled the kettle while I was waiting, so I'm making you some tea."

Agatha glared at her.

"I can make my own tea."

"I'm quite sure you can."

The redhead's eyes dropped briefly.

"...but I'd rather you didn't overdo it in your condition."

Fuck.
Her condition.
She was fucking pregnant.

The thought hit her all over again and this time her knees almost gave out.

Embarrassingly, she didn't swat Wanda away when the other omega caught her elbow.

A few minutes later Agatha found herself wrapped in a blanket on the couch while Wanda returned from the kitchen carrying two mugs of tea and a protein bar.

"This was all I could find."

The protein bar landed in Agatha's lap.

Agatha narrowed her eyes.

"I haven't had a chance to go grocery shopping… And Rio eats everything."

That earned a chuckle.

"Oh, I know. She once ate an entire box of my cereal in one sitting.”

Agatha sneered at her, her omega growling and hissing internally at the thought of Rio being in the company of another omega to know that kind of information. 

"So."

Wanda took a sip of tea.

"Is it Rio's?"

Agatha immediately choked as tea went down the wrong pipe.

Her eyes bulged.

She spluttered.

"What?! Do NOT ask me ridiculous questions while I am holding hot liquid, Wanda!"

Wanda didn’t even blink. 

“Well?”

This time Agatha did growl, out loud. 

“Of fucking course its Rio’s, bitch.”

"Okay."

The answer seemed to satisfy her.

Unfortunately she followed it up with:

"And what do you plan to do?"

Agatha froze.

Her face fell as she thought about it, the little life growing inside of her. Agatha was a fucking pro-choice activist for Christ's sake and had always said if she wasn’t ready then she would have no qualms getting rid of the clump of cells that had formed if needed. 

But now?

It was real. 

And it wasn’t just her decision to make. 

Would Rio even want this? Would she think it was too fast? Would Rio ask her to get rid of it?

She suddenly felt very protective of the little clump of cells and her hand drifted unconsciously over her stomach.

Her eyes began to sting.

Fuck, do NOT cry in front of her.

"Look," Wanda said quietly. "Whatever you decide, that's your choice."
Agatha stared into her tea.

"But you have to tell her."

The words were gentle but firm.

"I can't keep something like this from her."

"What if she doesn't—"

Agatha couldn't finish, the lump in her throat wouldn't let her.

Wanda's expression softened immediately.

"I know Rio."

Agatha looked up.

"And I know for a fact she will support whatever choice you make."

A tear escaped despite her best efforts.

Then another.

"It's all so sudden," she whispered. "We've barely started dating."

Wanda sat back slightly, studying her.

"Sometimes these things happen for a reason."

Agatha snorted weakly.

"Sounds like a fucking hallmark card.”

Wanda rolled her eyes fondly but continued.

“If you really love each other, you’ll figure it out.”

The room fell quiet.

“Do you love her, Agatha?”

The response came without hesitation as Agatha met Wanda’s eyes. 

“More than anything.”

Wanda didnt answer right away, she simply smiled a small knowing smile and nodded.

"Good."

Agatha swallowed hard.

"Because I've never seen Rio so in love before."

The tears she'd been fighting finally spilled over.

Wanda smiled softly.

"You are it for her, Agatha."

Fuck.

Why was she crying so much?

This was ridiculous.

Her hormones were already betraying her.

As if summoned by the thought, Wanda's phone suddenly began vibrating.

The redhead looked down at the screen and then immediately looked back up.

"It's Rio."

Agatha wiped at her face with her sleeve.

Of fucking course it was.

The poor idiot was probably climbing the walls, worried sick.

Wanda glanced between the phone and Agatha.

Then asked quietly:
"So... should I tell her to come talk to you?"

Agatha looked down at the mug cupped between her hands, at the steam curling from the tea and her shaking fingers around the porcelain.

She nodded, slowly.

"Yeah."

A deep breath.

"Yeah, fuck it.”

Because one way or another...

Shit was about to get really fucking real.
