“Mhm,” Agatha murmured, nodding against them. “I’m really good.”

Rio hummed, clearly pleased, and tightened their hold around her. Agatha let her eyes slip shut, breathing in slowly.

The future might still be uncertain, but right now, wrapped in Rio’s arms, everything feels exactly as it should be.
