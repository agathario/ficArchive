Everything about this seems like a good idea. 

From the way everything in the US has too high a sugar content, to the lack of a Nando’s on the corner of every street; from the fake coffeeshop smiles to the polite silence where an honest sneer should’ve sounded— Rio has long anticipated her move back to the UK.
