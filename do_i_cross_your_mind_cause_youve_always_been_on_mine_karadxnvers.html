"I can’t afford to be sick, not with a campaign to organize." Agatha walked toward the stove, grabbing the frying pan and placing it on the counter. "I tried to make our breakfast, my attempt at making pancakes didn’t go very well, so I made eggs with bacon."

Rio laughed.

"Why does that not surprise me?"

"Well, I’m not known for my culinary skills."

They ate in silence for a few minutes, and neither of them commented on the previous night. Rio didn’t want to think too much about the fact that she had spent the whole night with Agatha sleeping in her arms and the peace she felt.

"I’ll have to go to Italy." Agatha finally said. "For two weeks."

Rio didn’t take her eyes off her food, wanting to avoid looking at the model.

"I wanted to ask you to keep me updated on everything, about you, about the baby."

"I will."

Agatha gave a small smile.

Rio asked for borrowed clothes so she could go to work, since she hadn’t brought any change when she arrived at Agatha’s house. Of course, Agatha had lent her an outfit that could cost more than a month of Rio’s rent. She sincerely hoped no one at the office was into fashion.

"You look good wearing my clothes." Agatha commented when Rio appeared dressed, getting ready to leave.

Rio felt her face burn with embarrassment and Agatha laughed.

"Why are you embarrassed? You’ve already seen me naked."

If a hole opened in that room at that moment, Rio would certainly jump in without thinking twice.

"I asked Jack to drive you." Agatha continued.

"You don’t have to, I’ll take the subway."

"Rio, you came here in the middle of the night and took care of me, it’s the least I can do, please accept."

"Alright."

Rio needed to leave or she would be late, but before that she called Agatha and asked:

"Can I call you tonight?"

"Of course."

For now, they would get used to the small routine they were building, and that was enough for Rio.
